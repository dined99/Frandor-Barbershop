Our Story

About Frandor Barbershop

At Frandor Barber Shop, we believe getting a haircut should be more than just a routine—it should be an experience. Our talented team of barbers, led by Jerry, takes pride in providing precision cuts, fades, and beard trims, all while creating a welcoming, relaxed atmosphere where everyone feels at home.

From toddlers getting their first haircut to seasoned clients who travel from across town, we treat every guest with care and attention, ensuring they leave looking sharp and feeling great. With a friendly, approachable team, classic barber shop vibes, and a commitment to going above and beyond, we're proud to be the place where haircuts meet community. Stop in, sit back, and let us take care of you—you'll leave looking your best every time.
